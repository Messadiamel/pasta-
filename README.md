# pasta-
Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta
Ingredients (2 servings):

200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or whatever you have)

2 tablespoons butter

3 cloves garlic (minced)

1 cup cooking cream (or milk + 1 tbsp flour)

½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Salt & black pepper

Optional:

Chili flakes

Parsley

Chicken or mushrooms

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta

Bring salted water to a boil.

Add pasta and cook until al dente.

Keep ½ cup of the pasta water, then drain.

2. Make the sauce

In a pan, melt the butter.

Add the minced garlic → cook for 30 seconds.

Pour in the cream and stir.

Add Parmesan cheese and mix until it melts.

Add some pasta water to adjust thickness.

3. Combine

Add the cooked pasta to the sauce.

Season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.

Mix well so the pasta absorbs the sauce.

4. Serve

Top with extra Parmesan and chopped parsley.
